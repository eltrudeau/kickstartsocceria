Help Us Bring KickStart Soccer to Des Moines!

We’re exploring the launch of KickStart Soccer

A new indoor soccer program for kids aged 2–10 that’s about way more than just kicking a ball.

Our mission is simple: Build confident, active, and focused kids through soccer — indoors, year-round.

We use soccer as a tool to develop:

  • ✅ Motor skills & coordination
  • ✅ Teamwork & social confidence
  • ✅ Focus, discipline, and resilience
  • ✅ A love of movement and play

👶 Program Overview

  • 2–3: Movement, fun, bonding with parent
  • 3–5: Listening, balance, basic footwork
  • 6–10: Skill-building, teamwork, and confidence through gameplay

And because it's indoor and year-round, you don’t have to worry about weather cancellations or missed weeks.

💰 Pricing & Transparency

We’re considering a price of $139/month — including taxes and fees. This would include:

  • 🏟 Indoor facility access
  • 👟 Weekly classes with trained coaches
  • 💬 Monthly progress updates
  • 📅 Flexible scheduling and make-up options
