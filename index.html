More Than Just Kicking a Ball: The KickStart Difference

We believe soccer is a powerful tool for childhood development. Our age-specific curriculum is designed by experts to ensure your child isn't just learning soccer, but also growing valuable life skills.

What We'll Help Your Child Develop:

  • Enhanced Motor Skills & Coordination
  • Teamwork, Communication & Social Confidence
  • Improved Focus, Discipline, and Resilience
  • A Lifelong Love of Movement and Active Play
  • Listening Skills and Following Directions

Proposed Program Overview:

  • Mini Kickers (Ages 2–3): Introduction to movement, fun games, and parent bonding.
  • Little Strikers (Ages 3–5): Focus on listening, balance, basic ball control, and fun drills.
  • Junior Academy (Ages 6–10): Skill-building, teamwork, understanding game basics, and boosting confidence through positive coaching.

Year-Round Play, Rain or Shine! Our programs are planned for an indoor facility, meaning no weather cancellations and consistent fun and learning.
